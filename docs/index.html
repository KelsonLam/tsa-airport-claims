Data: official DHS TSA claims releases 2002–2015 (five files, schemas differ by era; only fields present in every era are used here — date, airport, claim type, claim site, disposition) joined to airport coordinates from the real Global Airport Database. This page is a companion to tsa_claims_analysis.ipynb, which covers the full cleaning, EDA, and modeling in one linear notebook; this page exists for the parts that benefit from hovering and filtering, the map and the category breakdowns, rather than trying to make a ROC curve interactive.
